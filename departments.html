Our Departments

Cliffside Police Department

The Cliffside Police Department is Cliffside's largest Law Enforcement Agency trusted by thousands of civilians. The CPD are enforced to serve and protect the city of Cliffside and surrounding areas.

Pineview County Sheriff's Office

The Pineview County Sheriff's Office is the largest law enforcement agency in Pineview County, trusted by thousands of residents. The PCSO is dedicated to serving and protecting the citizens of Pineview County and its surrounding communities.

Oakside Regional Police

The Oakside Regional Police is the primary law enforcement agency serving Oakside's regional areas. Trusted by thousands, the ORP is committed to maintaining safety, order, and security for all residents and visitors in the area.

Quantam Security

Quantam Security is a trusted leader in private security services, relied upon by countless clients to ensure safety and peace of mind. Dedicated to protecting people, assets, and communities, Quantam Security delivers professional solutions tailored to meet the highest standards of security and reliability.

Oakside Fire and Medical Services

Oakside Fire and Medical Services is the leading emergency response agency for Oakside and its surrounding areas. Trusted by the community, OFMS is committed to safeguarding lives and property through rapid fire suppression, advanced medical care, and exceptional rescue services.
