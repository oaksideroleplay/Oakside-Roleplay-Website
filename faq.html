Frequently Asked Questions

How can I be a moderator?

You can be a moderator by applying here.

I was banned. How can I appeal it?

If you were banned in-game, open a Ban Appeal ticket in our #assistance channel and a manager will review it. If you were banned on Discord, you can appeal it here.

How can I report a moderator, a department member, or a regular member?

If you believe a moderator, department member, or a regular member has violated a rule and would like to report them, open a report ticket corresponding to their role in #⁠assistance. You must have sufficient evidence or proof to support your claims, or your report will be discarded.

What are the perks to boosting?

We offer a large variety of booster perks for our valued members, including:

  • Exclusive server booster role
  • $5,000 daily in our #economy channel
  • Ability to use files in all channels
  • Get a special shoutout in #supporters
  • Ability to use external emojis/stickers
  • Access to booster only vehicles
  • Access to Oakside Regional Police and slicktops

What are the perks to Oakside+?

We offer a ton of different perks for Oakside+, including:

  • Exclusive @Oakside+ role
  • $10,000 daily in our #economy channel
  • Sneak peeks and early previews on future updates
  • Ability to use files in all channels
  • Get a special shoutout in #supporters
  • Ability to use external emojis/stickers

Have any more questions? Open a support ticket in our Discord server!
