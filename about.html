About Us

What is Oakside Roleplay?

Oakside Roleplay is a fictional roleplay server designed to deliver an immersive and unmatched roleplaying experience. With custom assets, liveries, and uniforms, our server allows players to dive into dynamic scenarios as civilians, criminals, law enforcement officers, and members of various other agencies.

What inspired Oakside Roleplay?

Oakside Roleplay was inspired by the vision of creating a community where players can engage in high-quality, creative, and inclusive roleplay. Founded in 2023 by @arc_xde, the server was re-established to bring a fresh start, combining innovative features with a commitment to fostering an enjoyable environment for everyone.

When did roleplay release?

Oakside Roleplay officially launched in 2023 and has continued to grow and evolve, offering new opportunities and features to enhance the roleplaying experience.

Our Background

Originally founded in 2023, Oakside Roleplay has undergone a revitalization to better serve its community. With over five departments, a dedicated staff team, and a range of diverse roleplay opportunities, the server has been rebuilt with a focus on providing something for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or new to the experience, Oakside Roleplay is here to welcome you.
